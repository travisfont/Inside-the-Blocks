# Supply Chain

## VeChain (VET & VTHO) 
MiCAR-compliant dual-token model separating value and fees, energy-efficient PoA/DPoS tailored for business scalability, and proven partnerships (Walmart, BMW) driving 14M+ on-chain addresses.

## IOTA (MIOTA)
Feeless, DAG-based for IoT/machine economy and traceability.

## Waltonchain (WTC)
Emphasizes RFID/IoT for traceability, focusing on manufacturing and less enterprise adoption.

## Hedera (HBAR)
Enterprise-grade with high-speed consensus (aBFT), council governance (Google, IBM), and supply chain apps.
